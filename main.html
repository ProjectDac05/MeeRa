About MeeRa:

MeeRa is a creative platform dedicated to sharing ideas, services, and beautiful imagery. We aim to inspire and connect through design and innovation. Dairy farming is one of the oldest and most important agricultural activities in the world. It involves the breeding and raising of dairy animals such as cows, buffaloes, goats, and sheep for the purpose of milk production. This milk is a vital source of nutrition and is used to make a variety of dairy products like cheese, butter, yogurt, and cream.
